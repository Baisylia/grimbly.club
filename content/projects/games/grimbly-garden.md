# Grimbly Garden

Grimbly Garden is a narrative-driven game I am developing where you build a garden to farm resources, and explore dungeons to befriend grimblies.

Grimbly Garden explores themes such as the magic circle, meta-awareness, agency, preservation, and pacifism. It also navigates LGBT+ topics and identity. The nature of the game’s world is slowly revealed over time as the player builds their garden, and these themes are portrayed through how the world and its characters react to these changes. 

For reference of what the game is like, some of its inspirations include Slime Rancher 2, Stacklands, Sonic
Adventure 2's Chao Gardens, Inscryption, DELTARUNE, and Cult of the Lamb.

A very early test prototype of the game is available [here](https://baisylia.itch.io/grimbly-garden), though just treat it as a proof of concept.
