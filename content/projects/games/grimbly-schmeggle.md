# Grimbly Schmeggle

Grimbly Schmeggle is a tower defense game inspired by Bloons about killing invading grimblies, made by me and the other Eye Pea Games members for an assignment.

It was a re-imagining of another game concept of mine, Ice Creamed, which was a tower defense game about desserts destroying breakfasts. Due to the time constraints of the assignment, Grimbly Schmeggle didn't come out as polished as I would have liked, and I will never publically release it as it contains some AI slop in it (yucky), but I think it was a fun little project.

After a few years, I created grimbly.club and eventually Grimbly Garden, throwing away the concept of grimblies being an invasive force to kill, rather re-branding them into a precious species to protect.
