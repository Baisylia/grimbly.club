# 20. The Twilight Forest
- Aesthetic: 1
- Code: 3
- Integration: 0
- Design: 1
- Fun: 2
Total: 7/25
Final Verdict: The Twilight Forest is one of the most popular dimension mods, for it is another very old mod. But don’t be fooled, its quality certainly does not match its notoriety. A dimension based around linear progressiondoes not fit Minecraft - a sandbox game - and a majority of the dimension looks indistinguishable from the overworld. It has poorly implemented ideas and subpar aesthetics that have not been improved in over a decade.
