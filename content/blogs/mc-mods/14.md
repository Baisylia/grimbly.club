# 14. Biomes O’ Plenty
- Aesthetic: 2
- Code: 3
- Integration: 2
- Design: 2
- Fun: 3
Total: 12/25
Final Verdict: Biomes O’ Plenty is a product of its time. It is full of bloat and low quality content that should have been removed or reworked long ago. It has some cool features and to its credit, a decent chunk of its aesthetics were redone to fit within recent versions better, but that isn’t enough to save it from being beaten out by many newer biome mods.
