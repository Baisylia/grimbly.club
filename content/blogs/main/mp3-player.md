# My MP3 Player

Streaming services are pretty terrible for a variety of reasons, but Spotify especially has been grinding my gears ever since I started using it.
I decided that enough was enough, and stopped using Spotify for my music completely.

I have instead been using an MP3 player to play my music, specifically a Snowsky Echo Mini, and I've
really been enjoying it. It's a pretty small device in the shape of a cassette player, which I think 
is quite cute, and I got mine in pink which is even cuter!
