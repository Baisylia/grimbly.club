# My Bag

Every now and then I see people (usually women who are obviously an artist and queer in some way) with a nice bag covered in badges and pins. As a closeted trans woman who repressed most of my fixations from the outside world for most of my life, I was always jealous of such bags, to the point where they became a point of gender envy for me.

After receiving a sage satchel as a random gift one day, I noticed it had nasty AI art printed on the main flap. I was going to throw it away until I realised that I had been out as trans for a while, and could finally use a bag like the ones I had been wishing for. But badges alone weren't going to cover the AI slop, so I sewed some fabric on top of it to place the badges on.

I then picked out my favourite badges from my existing collection from when I was younger, and ordered some new ones online for my more recent fixations. I think the final result turned out quite cool!
