About Terralink

Terralink is an analogue horror adventure game set in the same universe as Verditen, acting as a prequel of sorts.
It is a 3D first-person game with a pixelated rendering art style, where you collect mods for the company Terralink in order to earn a spot on their waiting list to get a robot body of your own. You can travel to various planets and locations to battle criminals and outcasts, while mixing and matching different mods to unlock different abilities and attacks.

The game is currently in the development stage, with the multiplayer framework being developed and the concept art / story being designed.
The game's lore has just about been finalised, but I won't leak too much information about it here, you'll have to play the game when it's released!

Multiplayer Functionality

I have begun developing the game in Unreal Engine, focusing on the multiplayer framework.
Multiple players can join/leave a game through LAN, and have the ability to use left hand and
right hand attacks.
