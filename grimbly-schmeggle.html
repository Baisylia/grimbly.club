About Grimbly Schmeggle

Grimbly Schmeggle is a tower defense game inspired by Bloons about killing invading grimblies, made by me and the other Eye Pea Games members for an assignment.
It was a re-imagining of another game concept of mine, Ice Creamed, which was a tower defense game about desserts destroying breakfasts.
Due to the time constraints of the assignment, Grimbly Schmeggle didn't come out as polished as I would have liked, so I started development on Grimbly Schmeggle 2.

The main idea of Grimbly Schmeggle 2 is that the enemies evolve as you choose certain strategies, meaning that no two games will be the same.
The game will have 5 tower classes, each containing 8 towers that can each be upgraded through 4 different skill paths.
Grimbly Schmeggle 2 has been on hold for a while due to its large scope, so once development continues the large amount of towers will likely be released in updates.
