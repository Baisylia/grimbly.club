About Grimbly Garden

Grimbly Garden is a roguelite creature collection game I am developing where you explore dungeons to befriend and rescue grimblies to keep and raise in your garden.

Grimbly Garden explores themes such as the magic circle, meta-awareness, agency, preservation, and pacifism. It also navigates LGBT+ topics and identity. The nature of
the gameâ€™s world is slowly revealed over time as the player completes runs, and these themes are portrayed through how the world and its characters react to these changes.

For reference of what the game is like, some of its inspirations include Slime Rancher 2, Sonic Adventure 2 Chao Gardens, Inscryption, DELTARUNE, and Cult of the Lamb.
