About Verditen

Verditen is an analogue horror card game set in space in the future, which has been in my game ideas backlog for a while.
It is a 3D first-person game with a pixelated rendering art style, where you verse robots in card games on various planets and spacecraft which you can travel to.
As you do so, there is a story to uncover about the world you've found yourself in, why all these robots are playing card games, and the identity of who you're playing as.

The game is currently in the ideation stage, with concept art being developed and the story / card gameplay being designed.
The game's lore has just about been finalised, but I won't leak too much information about it here, you'll have to play the game when it's released!
