Minecraft Mod Ratings


raspmary and I decided to rate Minecraft's most popular mods, and post them here to go with my personal update reviews.
We don't want to sound like we think we know everything, but with our combined time spent modding the game and working on our modpacks, if we can't tell a quality mod from one that is less than such, then I don't know who can.

While we try to rate mods from a somewhat objective standpoint, these ratings are not facts and are just our opinions.
None of our ratings are meant to belittle or attack the developers of these mods in any way.
The mod ratings are in order of mod popularity on CurseForge, excluding library/performance mods.
