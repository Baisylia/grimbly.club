Why grimbly.club?

I believe that everyone should have their own website, they are a great way to make portfolio of your work, and are a way to express yourself without the confines of social media.
That is why I made grimbly.club.

I also believe that the modern internet is cold, unfeeling, boring, uninspiring, predatory, and based around monetization.
That is why I chose the old web aesthetic for grimbly.club.

The idea of grimblies originated in a card game idea I had around 2021 or 2022. I wasn't satisfied with their usage in Grimbly Schmeggle, I felt as though they had more potential.
That is why I made grimblies the mascots for grimbly.club.
