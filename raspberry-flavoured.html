The best modpack to ever exist!!! And I truly mean it!!!

It is not created by me, rather by raspmary. Its design and quality are one of many reasons I fell in love with her. I was biased towards her because of the modpack, not the other way around, which is how you now it's good.

The pack is something of a vanilla+ experience, but with every feature and mechanic improved, modified in some way, or even removed. Don't let any feature removals scare you though, the gameplay experience is designed around these changes to maximise quality and fun.

I actually struggle to play any version of Minecraft that isn't this modpack now, I'm serious.
