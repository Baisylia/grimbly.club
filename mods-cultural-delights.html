Cultural Delights


A Farmer's Delight addon that adds food systems from around the world.

Available for Forge for 1.16.5, 1.18.2, 1.19.2, 1.20.1

Available for NeoForge and Fabric for 1.21.1

There is a separate Fabric port for other versions but it is rather unstable and not up to date.
