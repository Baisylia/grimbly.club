grimbly.club Info

The modern internet is cold, unfeeling, boring, uninspiring, predatory, based around corporate monetization, and full of ads and AI slop. Most people use the popular social media sites as their homes on the web, but I find the customisation to be lacking, and frankly they feel pretty dystopian.

I created grimbly.club to be more like the web of old, where people would make their own custom websites and decorate them however they wanted. I believe that everyone should have their own website, as they are a great way to express yourself online without the confines of social media.

The idea of grimblies originated in a card game concept I made in 2021. I re-used them in Grimbly Schmeggle, a game I worked on for a group project in 2022. I wasn't satisfied with their usage in this game, as I felt as though they had much more potential. This is why I made grimblies the mascots for my website. The grimblies are going to return once more in my new game Grimbly Garden.
